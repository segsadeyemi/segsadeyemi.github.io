About Me

I am currently an Entrepreneur in Training at Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology (MEST), Accra, Ghana. Before MEST, I worked as a growth executive at Hotels.ng. I also worked briefly as an analyst at a Lagos based investment bank. I studied Economics at the University of Lagos. I love to travel and I love adventure. I have strong interests in finance and technology.

Career

Let me quickly take you through my career trajectory.

  • 2009-2014

    University of Lagos.

    I studied Economics at the Uiversity of Lagos, Nigeria. I had amazing time in school, privileged to serve as my departmental president and participated in some extra curricular activities.

  • March 2012

    Intern at Mutual Benefit Properties

    I had a two month internship with the properties arm of Mutual Benefit Assurance Company Plc while in school. This exposed me to the property investment market in Nigeria.

  • May - August 2014

    Business Associate at Insidify.com

    Built strong interest in technology by working with the fantastic people in this start-up. I was able to further explore and learn more about the opportunities and leverage technology can and will drive.

  • January - March 2015

    Analyst at Union Capital Markets Ltd

    I did a little bit of everything in the short time here and it reaffirmed my believe in power of finance and technology in entrepreneurship. I learnt I a lot about valuations, equity research, private equity, stock trading and other similar skills at UCML.

  • April - June 2015

    Growth at Hotels.ng

    This is practical startup learning by doing. Very interesting and intense experience before I got invited to be an Eit at MEST.

  • August 2015 Till Date

    Entrepreneur in Training at MEST

    I am learning the core skills needed to build and scale software companies. I am doing this with amazing people and a very vibrant community. So happy to be here.
